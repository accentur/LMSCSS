8. Block level elements take 100 % width like <h1> tag.
9. Inline elements take the width its content needed. They do not have margin top/bottom.
10. Height of a tag element depends on its parent height. Use px instead of % .
11. Box-sizing is border-box, not content-box. We need width/height to accomdate for padding,border. Margin is not part of this.
12. display: inline-block. Child elements are behaving as inline. Their parent behaves as block kind (Eligibile for top/bottom rule).
